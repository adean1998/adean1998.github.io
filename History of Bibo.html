History of Bibo

Bibo, New Mexico (population 140) is an unincorporated community located in Cibola County. Bibo was founded in the 1880s by a family of German-Jewish immigrants. The town's diverse population now includes Hispanic, Indigenous, and Lebanese families.

Below are some things I like to do in Bibo:

  • Swimming in the irrigation ditch
  • Hiking in the hills
    • Hiking while watching the sunset
  • Eating carne adovada and tortillas with my family
Bibo boasts several tourist attractions, including:
  1. A bar
  2. An acequia (irrigation ditch)
  3. One horse
